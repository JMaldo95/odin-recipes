Pesto Pasta

Description

A recipe to make a pasta dish that is not only easy, but tasty too!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of pesto
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Stir in pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  4. Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes. Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.
  5. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.
